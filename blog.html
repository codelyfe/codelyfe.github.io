HTTP vs HTTPS

What is the difference?

HTTP - When you're sharing a network with a group or a coffee shop visiting this page would be ALRIGHT if you go to these pages. But strongly not recommend using any login features without going on your own network. Due to the fact that your data for login credentials will not be encrypted over the SHARED network. 

If you decide to visit any HTTP page just be mindful of who is on the network with you. Don't get scared.
